Fashion and Cosmetics

Starting my own business in the fashion and cosmetics industry is one of my biggest passions.

I am very passionate about starting a business in the fashion and cosmetics industry. I feel like as a child, I was naturally drawn to fashion and the beauty of art/makeup. I was introduced to fashion and cosmetics at a very young age by the females in my family and by watching celebrities. As a child when asked what did I want to be when I got older, I often replied with “fashion designer”.

In the beginning, watching older females in my family such as my mom and aunt led in sparking my passion for cosmetics and clothing. I would say that my biggest inspiration till this day would be myself. As I continue to learn more about myself, my opinion gradually changes and my aspect on fashion/cosmetics changes each day as I continue to learn more about the industry, giving me more inspiration day by day.
